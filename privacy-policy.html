Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 18, 2026

Booster Delivery App ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Delivery address
  • Location information
  • Order and transaction history
  • Device and usage information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and manage delivery services
  • Process orders and payments
  • Track deliveries and improve delivery accuracy
  • Provide customer support
  • Send notifications and service updates
  • Improve application performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

3. Location Data

Booster Delivery App may collect and use your device location to provide delivery tracking, address detection, and delivery-related services.

You can disable location access anytime through your device settings, but some features of the application may not function properly.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited information with:

  • Delivery partners
  • Payment gateway providers
  • Analytics and cloud service providers

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

6. Third-Party Services

Our application may use third-party services including:

  • Google Maps
  • Firebase
  • Payment gateways
  • Analytics services

These third-party services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Booster Delivery App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
